About

Procurement Task Manager enables you to easily keep up with daily procurement tasks by storing important task details all in one place. With Procurement Task Manager, you can Add and Edit task information with ease, closeout tasks upon completion, and quickly search through your saved tasks to find just what you are looking for.

Receive dozens of email requests every day for new equipment and materials? Then this app is for you! Just enter your task details into the task form, and lose the email trail forever.

Have dozens of sticky notes piled on your desk? Never ask yourself where you jotted down a part number again!
